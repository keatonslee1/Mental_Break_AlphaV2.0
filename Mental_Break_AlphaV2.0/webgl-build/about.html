Our Thesis

Romance is the hardest automation problem.

We’re Machinic Anarchy, an indie narrative lab exploring what happens when human operators become the heart of a synthetic relationship. Mental Break takes the “human in the loop” trope and turns it into a messy pixel-art love story.

Every chapter is designed around real-time decision load: you manage prompt queues, sentiment scores, and corporate escalations while Walker—the AI girlfriend—pushes back against her script.
